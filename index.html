About Us

Fact Sentry is a creative platform founded by two lifelong friends, Divyansh Gupta and Aditya Maurya. Since their school days, they shared a deep curiosity for exploring knowledge, ideas, and fascinating facts about the world. This channel is an effort to bring interesting information to a wider audience in the simplest, most engaging way possible.

From short, crisp fact-based videos to detailed explorations, Fact Sentry aims to inspire curiosity and encourage critical thinking. We believe in learning as a continuous journey, and through our work, we want to share that journey with you.

Every video, every fact, is a step towards building a community of curious minds who love exploring the unknown.
